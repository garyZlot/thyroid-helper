Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 29, 2025

Introduction

ThyroidCare (referred to as "we", "us", or "the App") takes your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information. By using this App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary to provide you with our services:

Health Data

  • Thyroid test indicators (FT3, FT4, TSH, A-TG, A-TPO, TG, etc.)
  • Test dates and medical report images
  • Medical history records and notes
  • Custom checkup reminder settings

Device Information (for technical support only)

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version number
  • Crash logs (if they occur)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Display and manage your health checkup data
  • Generate trend charts and data analysis
  • Provide checkup reminder functionality
  • Improve app performance and fix issues

3. Data Storage

All your health data is stored exclusively in your personal iCloud account. We do not store any of your health data on our servers.

  • Local Storage: Data is first saved on your device locally
  • iCloud Sync: If enabled, data automatically syncs to your iCloud account
  • Data Ownership: You have complete ownership and control
  • Cross-Device Sync: Devices using the same Apple ID can sync automatically

4. Data Sharing

We commit to:

  • ✓ Never sell your personal data
  • ✓ Never share your health data with third parties
  • ✓ Never use your data for advertising
  • ✓ Data only leaves the app when you use export features

We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising platforms.

5. Data Security

  • All data uses Apple iCloud's end-to-end encryption
  • The app uses Apple's Sign in with Apple for authentication
  • You can delete all data at any time through in-app settings

6. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data at any time
  • Export: Export your data in CSV or PDF format
  • Delete: Delete any or all data at any time
  • Portability: Transfer data to other apps via export

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information without permission, please contact us and we will take steps to remove such information.

8. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be notified within the app. Continued use of the app indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
